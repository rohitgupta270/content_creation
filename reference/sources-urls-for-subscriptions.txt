TIPS FOR SUBSCRIBING
====================

1. Substack newsletters: Click the email icon and subscribe directly
2. RSS feeds: Copy the URL into Feedly or your favorite RSS reader
3. Blog feeds: Most publications have /blog or /insights sections with RSS feeds
4. Podcasts: Copy to Spotify, Apple Podcasts, or your podcast app
5. Email newsletters: Create a dedicated email filter or folder to keep these organized

RECOMMENDED TOOLS:
- Feedly (aggregates all sources)
- Substack (subscribe to individual newsletters)
- Google News (create sections for each topic)
- Twitter/X (follow the newsletter authors)
