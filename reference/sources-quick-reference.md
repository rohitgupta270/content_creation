# Industry Sources: Quick Reference

**For the impatient:** Here are the TOP 5 sources per category that should be in your daily/weekly rotation.

---

## AI & ML (Top 5 Must-Read)

1. **The Batch** (Andrew Ng) — https://www.deeplearning.ai/the-batch/
   - Weekly, balanced technical + business implications
   
2. **Superhuman** (Rahul Vohra) — https://superhuman.com/blog
   - AI changing product design (not just "add AI")
   
3. **Import AI** (Jack Clark) — https://importai.substack.com/
   - Research + product implications, cuts through hype
   
4. **AI Breakfast** (Ed Toner) — https://aibreakfast.substack.com/
   - 3x per week, high signal-to-noise ratio
   
5. **Gradient Descent** (Ben Lorica) — https://gradientdescent.io/
   - AI, data, product strategy with real-world context

**Time commitment:** 15 minutes/day for all 5

---

## Payments & Fintech (Top 5 Must-Read)

1. **FinTech Inside** — https://fintechinside.substack.com/
   - Weekly, regulation + product moves + market trends
   
2. **Fintech Futures** — https://www.fpinsights.com/
   - Deep industry analysis on Open Banking, PSD3, real-time payments
   
3. **Paytech Daily** — https://paytechdaily.substack.com/
   - Daily curated news with PM lens
   
4. **The Block Crypto** (David Z. Morris) — https://www.theblockcrypto.com/
   - Crypto/blockchain implications for next-gen payment infrastructure
   
5. **Embedded Finance Digest** — https://embeddedfinance.substack.com/
   - Weekly on embedded finance + fintech platforms (your sweet spot)

**Time commitment:** 15 minutes/day for all 5

## How to Use This

### Daily Rhythm (15-20 minutes)

- Scan: The Batch, FinTech Inside, PhocusWire, 1 PM source
- Look for patterns: What's being discussed across sources?
- If you find a strong signal → save it with the template below

### Weekly Deep Dive (45 minutes)

- Read full articles from 1-2 sources per category
- Listen to one Lenny podcast episode
- Capture ideas in `outputs/trend-signals-YYYY-MM-DD.md`

### When You Find a Signal

Use this template to capture it:

```markdown
## [Signal Title]
**Source:** [Newsletter/Publication]
**Date:** [YYYY-MM-DD]
**Category:** [AI / Payments / Travel / PM]

**Signal:** [What's happening — the raw signal]
**Why it matters:** [Business or product implication]
**PM angle:** [How Rohit can approach this as content]
**Post type:** [Viral replication / Trend surfing / Pain point]
**Example hook:** [First line of potential post]
```

### Example

```markdown
## NDC Adoption Accelerating in Asia-Pacific
**Source:** PhocusWire
**Date:** 2026-06-04
**Category:** Travel

**Signal:** Singapore Airlines, ANA, and China Southern moving NDC distribution rapidly. Bypassing GDSs. Direct pricing control.

**Why it matters:** Airlines taking back control of pricing and customer data. OTAs losing margin power. Loyalty programs becoming primary differentiator.

**PM angle:** "The distribution shift everyone's talking about is actually a discovery problem. Here's how to think about it as a PM."

**Post type:** Viral replication (take a viral travel post structure + adapt to NDC)

**Example hook:** "The airline retailing revolution isn't about technology. It's about who controls the customer relationship."
```
